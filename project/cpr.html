CPR Awareness

Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation can save lives during emergencies

Steps of CPR

Check if the person is responsive and breathing.
Call emergency services immediately.
Place hands in the center of the chest.
Push hard and fast (100-120 compressions per minute).
Allow chest to return fully between compressions.
Continue CPR until medical help arrives.
